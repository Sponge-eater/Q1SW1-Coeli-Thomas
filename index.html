catfish facts and varities

today i will talk about catfish varities and facts about each of my favorites

    my favorite catfish
  • Wels Catfish

    the largest variety of catfish which is known to grow upto 2m in length and 90kg in weight it has a distinctly wide mouth that looks somewhat pouty it is also quite long compared to its width
  • Redtail Catfish

    the red-tail is an stealthy ambush predator as well as one of the largest catfish varieties in the world it has a destinct red tail and white underbelly.
  • Mekong Giant Catfish

    the mekong is the 2nd largest variety of catfish in the world with its defining trait being eyes located slightly below mouth level as well as a pale white-yellow underbelly.
  • Channel Catfish

    the channel catfish is the most common catfish in north america and its often eaten
    it is quite common looking so there isnt many ways to distinguish it from other catfish other than using it as a reference point
  • Goonch Catfish/Giant Devil Catfish

    The goonch is an odd case as a folk-legend claims that the goonch had consumed people in the kali river which is what made people name it the giant devil
